Workout Design

You will receive a customised programme tailored to your individual needs and designed to help you reach your specific goals. My training sessions are engaging and challenging, focusing on building strength, endurance, and balance. I gradually increase the workout intensity of each session and ensure you reach your goals by continuously reaching new levels of achievement and satisfaction. Whether you are an athlete looking to improve your performance, or simply someone looking to tone up and feel great, I can help you to optimise your performance in your chosen sport, tone your muslces, develop your core strength, mobility and overall well-being by training you with a mix of functional exercises and conditioning drills.

Fitness Assessment

I will provide a comprehensive analysis of your physical capabilities and goals. This assessment typically involves meal track and anthropometrics, measuring various aspects of physical fitness, including cardiovascular endurance, muscular strength, flexibility, body composition, and balance.

Services

Functional & Corrective Exercise

A form training that mimics movements used in daily life or sports activities, such as lifting, pushing, pulling, bending, twisting, and squatting. Also, exercises that address muscle imbalances, improve posture, and prevent injuriesImproves strength, balance, flexibility, agility, and coordination.

Aerobics

Exercise that involves rhythmic and continuous movements designed to increase heart rate, endurance, and overall fitness.

Warrior

High-intensity strength and conditioning exercises with elements of functional movement, aimed at improving overall fitness, strength, and agility. Promoting discipline, resilience, and confidence.

Plans-only

Customized workout plans to individuals without providing in-person training or coaching.

Legs, Bums & Tums

Toning and strengthening the muscles in the legs, buttocks, and abdominal area.

Bootcamp

High-intensity interval training (HIIT) that combines various exercises such as strength training, cardio, and calisthenics, designed to improve overall fitness, strength, and endurance.

Sports-Specific Training

Exercise program designed to improve the physical skills and abilities required for a particular sport or athletic activity.

Summer Body

A time-limited fitness program designed to help you improve your physical health, lose weight, and tone your body in preparation for the summer season.
